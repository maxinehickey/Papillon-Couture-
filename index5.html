Papillon Couture was created by Maxine Hickey for her SCORE project in the Spring of 2020. Through this project, Maxine was able to combine her two passions: coding and fashion.

This project was first created out of spite for not winning best dressed of her class, but it then became something that she poured her heart into. She wanted to create an elegant website that captured her sense of style that resembled haute couture.
